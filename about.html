About Gardenor

I'm Breno Martin, the founder of the project Sweet Home. With a passion for transforming spaces and a wealth of experience in general services—especially in gardening, landscaping, painting, and finishing—I take pride in delivering high-quality results.

Who Am I

Over the years, I’ve worked both independently and with major companies like the Multi-National Gerdau, always striving for excellence in every project. With a passion for problem-solving and innovation, I combine my technical expertise with hands-on skills to deliver efficient, high-quality services. I also have a deep interest in languages, speaking Portuguese, and other languages, while actively developing fluency in Russian. My goal is to create and enhance outdoor spaces with professionalism, creativity, and sustainability, always exceeding expectations.

My Value

I strongly believe that every project, no matter the size, deserves utmost attention and care. My values are rooted in quality, sustainability, and a commitment to every client. I approach each landscaping and gardening project with a dedication to preserving the environment, using eco-friendly methods and materials whenever possible. My goal is not just to create beautiful outdoor spaces, but to build long-lasting, harmonious environments that contribute positively to nature.

Our Assessment

Our team evaluates key aspects like soil health, drainage, and design potential to create a tailored plan that fits your needs. We combine expert insights with your preferences to deliver exceptional, sustainable results.
